# Governance

Klein for Ray is an independent project. This document does not claim
acceptance, affiliation, or endorsement by Ray's maintainers.

## Decision making

- Routine changes are accepted by maintainer review and passing required checks.
- Public API, compatibility, licensing, governance, and security changes require
  two maintainer approvals once the project has at least two maintainers.
- Decisions prefer lazy consensus. Unresolved technical disputes are documented
  in an issue with alternatives, evidence, and a final maintainer rationale.
- Maintainers must disclose conflicts of interest and recuse themselves where
  independence could reasonably be questioned.

## Maintainers

The current maintainer is:

- [Yu Chen (`@yuchen-ecnu`)](https://github.com/yuchen-ecnu)

Maintainers are contributors with a sustained record of sound technical and
community judgment. Existing maintainers nominate new maintainers in a public
issue. Removal for inactivity should follow at least 30 days' notice; removal for
security or conduct reasons may be immediate and privately documented.

The repository's CODEOWNERS file records the maintainers responsible for final
review. Governance changes are proposed and discussed in public issues.
